What It Does

A full restaurant, in one desktop app.

Pass covers every screen a working restaurant actually needs — order to bill, kitchen to counter, staff to statement — and does it whether the internet is up or not.

01

Money & Payments

Bills the auditor accepts. Payments the counter closes fast.

  • GST-compliant billing — CGST/SGST/IGST breakdown, GSTIN & FSSAI on every invoice
  • Split payments — cash, UPI, card and loyalty points on a single bill
  • Dynamic UPI QR codes — Razorpay, PhonePe, Paytm or plain static UPI, auto-confirmed every 3 seconds
  • Cash change calculator with colour-coded display
  • Auto-print on payment — configurable per station
  • Post-billing rectification with manager-PIN authentication
  • Bill void with reason and staff tracking
  • Configurable prefixes and audit-safe sequential numbering
02

Order Operations

Dine-in, takeaway, delivery — all on one screen, all in one flow.

  • Visual table floor plan — sections (AC Room, Garden, Rooftop) with colour-coded statuses
  • Quick Order Entry with keyboard type-ahead search
  • KOT printing to any thermal printer with auto-numbered tickets
  • WhatsApp order notifications — pre-formatted, one click
  • Table transfer, hold, cancel with reason tracking
  • Auto-generated order numbers (ORD-YYYYMMDD-NNN)
  • Customer phone lookup for returning guests
  • Live order status — placed → preparing → ready → served
03

Menu & Inventory

Hierarchical menus, real recipes, stock that deducts itself.

  • Categories with colour coding, item counts and one-click availability toggle
  • Size variants (Half/Full, Small/Large) with individual pricing
  • Add-on modifiers (Extra Cheese, No Onion) with pricing deltas
  • Per-item tax rate (0%, 5%, 12%, 18%)
  • Cost-price tracking for real profit margin calculation
  • AI Menu Reader — photograph a physical menu, items extracted automatically
  • Recipe management — link menu items to ingredients, auto-deduct stock on orders
  • Low-stock alerts, vendor tracking, wastage log
04

Staff, Payroll & Loyalty

Team management on the same machine as the till. No extra tool.

  • Role-based access — Owner, Manager, Cashier, Waiter, Kitchen · 14 granular permissions
  • 4-digit PIN authentication — no passwords to remember
  • Manager PIN override for sensitive operations
  • Monthly attendance grid with P/A/H/L keyboard shortcuts
  • Auto-calculated salary sheets — gross, advances, net pay, Excel export
  • Salary advances with deduction tracking
  • Phone-based customer database with visit count and total spend
  • Loyalty points earning + redemption as a payment mode
05

Intelligence & Reports

Numbers with an opinion. AI that reads your restaurant back to you.

  • Daily business insights — AI compares today vs yesterday with actionable recommendations
  • 7-day sales forecast based on 30-day historical patterns
  • Menu pricing advisor — flags underperformers and suggests changes
  • Marketing message generator — 3 WhatsApp/SMS variants for promos, festivals, launches
  • Daily sales summary, date-range and item-wise reports
  • GST report — monthly bill-level CGST/SGST/IGST breakdown
  • Profit & Loss — categorised expenses, net margin
  • All reports export to Excel and PDF
06

Infrastructure & Trust

The quiet layer that keeps the loud parts running.

  • Fully offline — SQLite database on your hardware, no internet required to run the restaurant
  • Multi-terminal LAN sync — cashier, kitchen, waiter stations on the same WiFi, zero config
  • Optional cloud backup — asynchronous replication across all data tables
  • Auto-updates — download in the background, install silently on quit
  • Thermal printing — 80mm KOT + bill printers, silent (no print dialog)
  • Hardware-bound license with offline verification, HMAC-signed keys
  • Full theme system — dark and light, instant toggle
  • 2-minute onboarding wizard — restaurant running fast

Answers

Questions restaurants ask before signing.

What actually happens during the 30-day free trial?
We install the full product on your counter machine, run the 2-minute onboarding wizard with you, and train your team in a live walkthrough — all included. You use every feature for 30 days, exactly as a paid customer would. If Pass doesn't earn its place, you owe nothing and we uninstall it. No card is taken up front.
Do I really need no internet at all?
Correct — 100% of core operations (orders, KOT printing, billing, payments, reports, backups) work fully offline. Internet is only used for optional cloud backup, AI insights, silent product updates and WhatsApp order notifications. A restaurant with a completely dead connection can serve every table without missing a bill.
How does multi-terminal work?
One machine acts as the host — usually the counter — and every other terminal on the same WiFi (kitchen KOT screen, waiter tablet, second cashier) connects to it automatically. No IP addresses, no port forwarding. If the host goes down, you keep taking orders on the other terminals and they sync back when it returns.
Can I use my own thermal printer?
Yes. Pass supports any 80mm thermal receipt printer — USB, network, or Windows default. You can set different printers for KOT and bills. Silent printing means no print dialog interrupts a busy counter.
How is this different from a cloud POS I already pay for?
Cloud POS is a rental — you pay every month, forever, and the moment your internet drops your restaurant stops taking bills. Pass is a one-time annual license. Your database lives on your machine. Your data is yours. Your restaurant works when the WiFi doesn't.
What if a device fails or the counter dies mid-shift?
If you're running multi-terminal, the other terminals keep taking orders. Your data is on local SQLite with WAL mode — power cuts do not corrupt it. Optional cloud backup replicates all 17 data tables asynchronously, so a full hardware loss still recovers on a fresh install.
Can I get all my data out if I want to leave?
Yes. Every report and dataset — menu, orders, bills, staff, inventory, customers — exports to Excel and PDF directly from the app. Your database file itself is standard SQLite, portable to anywhere. No data-ransom situation, ever.
How does the referral discount work?
Refer another restaurant to Pass. When they sign up as a paid customer, you — the referring restaurant — get a flat ₹1,500 off, credited immediately (not held until renewal). Stackable: three successful referrals means ₹4,500 in credit against your account. The referred restaurant pays the standard ₹7,500 for their first year but is free to refer their own restaurants and earn the same discount themselves.
What happens on renewal?
Renewals are billed at the standard flat ₹7,500 per restaurant per year, regardless of how you got in (trial, referral, or direct). No auto-charge — we contact you before renewal and you confirm. No usage tiers or surprise hikes.
