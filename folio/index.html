Our Story

Three generations of hospitality.
One software team.

Folio wasn't drawn on a whiteboard by product managers who'd never worked a hotel floor. Our family has been in Indian hospitality for three generations — grandfather, father, and now us — running and operating properties across formats and regions.

Somewhere along the way, we became entrepreneurs and technologists too. Which meant we started noticing the same gap everywhere: the software our own front desks used was excellent at the front-of-house, and completely silent about everything that happened after the guest walked into their room.

Room service orders on notebook pages. Dues tracked in a WhatsApp chat. Bills reconstructed at checkout from memory. Not because owners didn't care — because no one had built the layer.

We built Folio the way we would have wanted it in our own properties: staff-first, owner-honest, and operational under any conditions — patchy internet, power cuts, staff turnover, month-end audits. Every feature answers a question one of us had asked as an operator first.

What It Does

Not a stripped-down MVP.
The full room-service operation.

Folio covers the entire lifecycle of a room-service business — from an 11pm order call to a month-end audit — including the messy real-world edge cases most billing tools quietly ignore.

01

Money & Accounting

Everything the auditor asks for. Everything the owner wants to see.

  • Dues / pay-later — corporate guests sign, room stays free for the next guest; aging buckets (0–7 / 8–30 / 31–60 / 60+ days)
  • Bad-debt write-off with automatic GST reversal — books stay clean
  • Refunds with a proper audit trail, auto-adjusts revenue
  • Bill-level cost override — for negotiated lump-sum deals with restaurant partners
  • Cash-basis P&L — reports show what you actually collected, not what you billed
  • Multi-restaurant partner tracking — profit visible per partner
  • Composite GST scheme (single rate) with per-item override toggle
  • Rupee rounding with a transparent "Round Off" line on every bill
02

Operations

Speed at the front desk. Nothing to remember, nothing to look up.

  • Fully offline — bill and take orders with no internet at all
  • 80mm thermal printer support out of the box — customer & hotel copies printed together
  • WhatsApp bill share — one click, auto-fills guest phone
  • Bill reprint anytime, no time limit
  • Bulk billing for group checkouts and corporate stays
  • Day close + shift handover — auto-printed, cash reconciliation built in
  • CSV import/export for rooms, menu, and dues — accountant-friendly
03

Data Safety

The part that convinces owners. Nothing lost, ever.

  • AES-256 encrypted database, locked to the machine's hardware
  • Hourly auto-backup — 48 backups retained, 2 full days of history
  • Auto-recovery from backup if the database is ever corrupted
  • Automatic crash reporting — we see issues and push fixes before you complain
  • Silent auto-updates — background download, install on restart
  • In-app diagnostics with one-click error report to support
04

Growth & Reporting

Numbers that tell you where the money is — and where it leaks.

  • Room-wise revenue — which room brings in the most spend
  • Item-wise reports — top sellers, thinnest margins
  • Handover log — every shift, every till, accounted for
  • Order-status tracking — received → preparing → ready → delivered
05

Trust & Compliance

The quiet layer that stops trouble before it starts.

  • Manager PIN for sensitive actions — day close, write-offs, rectification
  • Sequential bill numbering with your own prefix — GST-audit safe
  • Bill correction with versioning — new bill, linked to original, reason logged
  • Bundled legal docs — EULA, ToS, Privacy, Liability shown in-app
  • Hardware-bound licensing — no piracy, no shared installs
  • 2-minute onboarding wizard — hotel operational fast

Answers

Questions hotels ask before signing.

What actually happens during the 7-day free trial?
We install the full product on your hotel's machine, run the 2-minute onboarding wizard with you, and train your front desk in a live walkthrough — all included. You use every feature for 7 days, exactly as a paid customer would. If Folio doesn't earn its place, you owe nothing and we uninstall it. No card is taken up front.
Do I really need no internet at all?
Correct — 100% of core operations (orders, billing, dues, printing, reports, backups) work fully offline. Internet is only used for silent product updates and optional WhatsApp bill sharing. A hotel with a completely dead connection can operate all day without missing a bill.
What happens if the computer crashes or a power cut hits mid-bill?
The database is transactional and auto-backed-up every hour, 48 backups deep. On a hard crash Folio recovers from the most recent good backup on next launch. In over a month of live production use, we've had zero data loss on power cuts.
Can I get all my data out if I want to leave?
Yes. Rooms, menu, orders, bills, and dues export to CSV directly from the app — no support ticket, no data-ransom situation. Your data is yours.
What hardware do I need?
A Windows machine (7 / 8 / 10 / 11), an 80mm thermal printer for bills (most hotels already have one), and — optionally — internet for bill-share and updates. Nothing else. No server, no cloud subscription, no per-terminal licensing.
How does the referral discount work?
Refer another hotel to Folio. When they sign up as a paid customer, you — the referring hotel — get a flat ₹1,500 off, credited immediately (not held until renewal). Stackable: three successful referrals means ₹4,500 in credit against your account. The referred hotel pays the standard ₹6,500 for their first year but is free to refer their own hotels and earn the same discount themselves.
What happens on renewal?
Renewals are billed at the standard flat ₹6,500 per property per year, regardless of how you got in (trial, referral, or direct). No auto-charge — we contact you before renewal and you confirm. No usage tiers or surprise hikes.
Is Folio built by the same team behind The Big Brand Network?
Yes. Folio is built by The Binary Brain Network, the software division of The Big Brand Network — a full-service brand ecosystem based in Mumbai, operating across Delhi NCR and pan-India. Trademark registered, MSME certified.
