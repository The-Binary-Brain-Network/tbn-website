The Gap

Your hotel software handles check-in.
Nobody built the bit after.

Most hotel management systems are excellent at bookings, rooms, and front-desk operations. Almost none of them handle what happens when a guest calls down for food at 11pm — so it ends up on a notepad, a WhatsApp message, or nowhere at all.

Real Deployment

A 34-room mid-range hotel near Jogeshwari/Andheri, Mumbai runs Menson for their day-to-day hotel management — but Menson has no room service module. Folio now handles every in-room order, kitchen ticket, and itemized bill alongside it. Live for over a week.

What It Does

Everything between an order and a bill.

Not a stripped-down MVP — Folio handles the full lifecycle of a room service operation, including the messy edge cases that actually happen on a hotel floor.

01 / TABS

Multi-room tabs

Every room runs its own open tab — guest name, check-in date, running total. Unlimited simultaneous tabs across different rooms, with duplicate-tab protection per room.

02 / ORDERS

Orders against any tab

Add multiple line items at once from your menu. Each tracks cost price, selling price, and per-unit profit automatically — no separate spreadsheet needed.

03 / INVENTORY

Auto stock deduction

Link a menu item to an inventory item once. Every order placed deducts the right quantity automatically — reversed cleanly if an order is cancelled.

04 / BILLING

GST-ready itemized bills

CGST + SGST calculated automatically when enabled. Sequential bill numbering with your own prefix. Customer copy and hotel copy printed together.

05 / RECTIFY

Bill correction, properly versioned

Made a mistake on a printed bill? Rectify it — Folio creates a new versioned bill linked to the original with a reason logged, never silently overwritten.

06 / BULK

Bulk billing across tabs

Finalize and print combined bills for multiple rooms in one operation — built for group bookings and corporate stays settling together.

07 / PAYMENTS

Partial & full payments

Take cash, card, or UPI payments against a tab at any point — partial or full — with running paid totals and balance due always visible.

08 / REPORTS

Day-close & handover reports

Printable end-of-day summaries, revenue by room and by item, and a dedicated shift handover report so nothing gets lost between staff changes.

09 / SECURITY

PIN-protected access

A 4–6 digit PIN keeps the till honest. Combined with a hardware-locked license, Folio stays tied to the machine it was issued for.

Getting Started

Set up once. Runs itself after.

No IT department, no server, no monthly maintenance call. Folio is installed, configured, and left to run.

01

Install & activate

We install Folio on the hotel's machine and issue a hardware-locked license key. No admin password required — runs as a standard per-user install.

Hotel details, rooms, and partner restaurants are set up directly in the app — no spreadsheets, no support ticket. Takes about 20 minutes for a property your size.
